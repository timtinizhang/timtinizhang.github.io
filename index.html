Hi! You can call me Tim.

I am a final-year undergraduate student majoring in International and Public Affairs at the University of Macau, where I received a full scholarship. I also serve as the Head of Academics of the Youth Association of International Affairs. Beginning in July 2027, I will join the Australian National University as a Master of Economics student.

My research interests lie in applied microeconomics, with a focus on development economics, political economy, international economics, and labor economics.

I have completed extensive training in quantitative and empirical methods with top-level academic performance, alongside long-term engagement and substantial hands-on experience in political science and political economy. This background has equipped me with a solid foundation in literature review, research design, and data analysis, with methodological strengths in econometric and statistical methods, formal theory, R programming, computational text analysis, survey experiments, and social network analysis.
